About SR ShahRose Fragrance

At Shah Rose, we believe fragrance is more than a scent — it’s a signature of the soul. Inspired by timeless elegance and crafted for the modern man, our perfumes are a blend of heritage, passion, and sophistication.

Each bottle tells a story — from the bold freshness of bergamot and mandarin orange, the subtle sweetness of apple and cassis, to the spicy depth of nutmeg and the captivating base of patchouli, musk, amber, and coffee. These carefully chosen ingredients create a scent that lingers with confidence, mystery, and masculine grace.

Founded with a vision to redefine luxury fragrance in Pakistan and beyond, Shah Rose embodies excellence in every detail — from design to essence. Whether you're stepping into a boardroom or an evening of romance, Shah Rose ensures you leave a lasting impression.
